Campus Involvement

Spring 2026

I am still a member of Ithaca's Club Soccer Team. We met three times a week in season (Mon, Tues, Thurs, from 8:00-10:00pm) for practices. We also played one to two games per weekend. In the off-season, we often meet at the fitness center and play indoor soccer. We tend to meet every day at 8:00 pm, permitting attendance. At practices, we create strategies for games and we build chemistry within the team. We play a mixture of small-sized games along with larger scrimmages. For games, we travelled to other schools in teammates' cars, as well as hosted games on Ithaca's Higgins Field. We played in an indoor league in the winter, which helped us stay active while the weather prevented outside play.

I am also a tutor for Chemistry, Biology and Physics at Ithaca College. I enjoy tutoring because it gives me the oppertunity to get paid for teaching my peers. It is also a great way to make sure I don't lose any knowledge I have gained.
